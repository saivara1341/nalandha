About Us

A Tradition of Excellence

Nalanda High School, located in Shivaji Nagar near Radio Station, Nizamabad, has provided English-medium co-educational training from Nursery to Class 10 since 1980.

Our curriculum equips students to excel in board examinations and competitive higher studies with dedicated faculty mentoring.
